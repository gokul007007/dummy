Courses offered

CIVIL ENGINEERING

The department of civil engineering is one of the best departments in the state with state-of-the-art laboratories and well qualified, experienced faculty members. The department is offering both UG Course (B.E) with an intake of 120 students and PG (M.Tech) in structural engineering with an intake of 24 students affiliated to VTU, Belagavi. The department accredited twice by NBA, the department is recognized as the Visvesvaraya Technological University research center and offers M.Sc(Engg)/Ph.D. program. The department so far bagged 29 ranks since from the inception, out of that two first ranks from the VTU Belagavi.

MECHANICAL ENGINEERING

The department of Mechanical Engineering is the oldest department of the institute since its inception established in the year 1980 with well qualified, experienced faculty members. and accredited twice by NBA and currently affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belagavi. The department offers undergraduate courses in Mechanical Engineering with 120 intakes, a post-graduate program in Thermal Power Engineering, and Machine Design. Department is also recognized as a research center for carrying out research activity leading to Doctoral degrees and M.Sc., Engineering by research.

COMPUTER SCIENCE & ENGINEERING

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering is established in the year 1986 with well qualified, experienced faculty members. The department is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belagavi, and accredited twice by National Broad of Accreditation, New Delhi. The department offers UG Course (B.E) with an intake of 120 students, PG(M.Tech) Courses with an intake of 18 students, and facilitates R&D through VTU recognized research center. The department is constantly providing career guidance for the students in order to achieve good university results and has excellent placement records.

ELECTRONICS & COMMUNICATION ENGINEERING

The Electronics and Communication Engineering department started in the year 1981. The department is under the affiliation of Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belagavi, Department offers regular full-time UG, PG and research program. The Department has a present intake of 120 students and accredited twice by the NBA . It has pioneered in carving the careers of its students and helping to acquire the necessary skills required for continuous growth with a significant impact on society.

ELECTRICAL & ELECTRONICS ENGINEERING

The Department of Electrical & Electronics Engineering is started in the year 1980 and accredited twice by NBA. The program affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belagavi. It has pioneered in carving the careers of its students and helping to acquire the necessary skills required for continuous growth with a significant impact on society. The Program has an intake of 60 for Undergraduate students, 24 for Postgraduate students and the Program having Research center and is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belagavi.
